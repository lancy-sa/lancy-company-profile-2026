Product Portfolio & Brands
Four world-class brands. One vision.
Exclusively clinically validated — no compromises on quality, safety, or results
Pluryal
HA Fillers · Biostimulators · PN · Mesotherapy
Premium Luxembourg-made portfolio covering the full spectrum of regenerative aesthetics: HA fillers, biostimulators, Polynucleotides (PN), and the Mesoline mesotherapy line. Pluryal also launched meso'me by Pluryal — an at-home microneedling sub-brand bringing professional-grade skin care into patients' daily routines. Lancy pioneered the PN category in Greece through Pluryal, making Greece one of Pluryal's global reference markets.
Revitrane
HA Fillers · Skin Boosters
A focused range of premium HA fillers and skin boosters targeting skin hydration, texture and overall skin quality. Designed for natural, refined results across all skin types.
Epline / Yourline
PDO / PLLA Threads
Non-surgical lifting and contouring via PDO and PLLA thread technology. Full face & body protocols with dedicated LMT certification program.
Restylane
Former Partner · HA Filler · #2 Global
Lancy served as official distributor of Restylane — the world's most trusted HA filler range — for three years (until 2024). During this partnership, Lancy earned the Gold Award for Best Eye Filler (Restylane Eyelight) at the Medical Beauty Awards 2023. A defining chapter in our growth story.
