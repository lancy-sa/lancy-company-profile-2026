Restylane
Former Partner · HA Filler · #2 Global
Lancy served as official distributor of Restylane — the world's most trusted HA filler range — for three years (until 2024). During this partnership, Lancy earned the Gold Award for Best Eye Filler (Restylane Eyelight) at the Medical Beauty Awards 2023. A defining chapter in our growth story.

HA Filler · Biostimulators
Premium Luxembourg-made HA fillers and Pluryal Densifiers — Greece's first Polynucleotides line, pioneered exclusively by Lancy. Ideal for natural rejuvenation.

Epline / Yourline
PDO / PLLA Threads
Non-surgical lifting and contouring via PDO and PLLA thread technology. Full face & body protocols with dedicated LMT certification program.
